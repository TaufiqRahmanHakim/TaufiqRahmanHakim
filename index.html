Personal Project

Margin of Error

Where Mathematics Meets Strategy

Margin of Error is a revolutionary roguelike deck-building game that transforms mathematical concepts into thrilling strategic gameplay. This isn't just another card game – it's a mathematical adventure where every decision matters and every calculation could be the difference between victory and defeat.

🎲 Roguelike Mechanics

Procedurally generated levels ensure no two playthroughs are identical

🃏 Unique Card System

Cards with numbers 0-9 and operators like +, -, ×, φ (phi), and more

🧠 Mathematical Puzzles

Build complex equations and solve challenging mathematical problems

⚡ Strategic Depth

Combine logic, strategy, and mathematical thinking for victory

Robo Rescue

Project MSIB Agate Academy

In Robo-Rescue: City Chaos, players take on the role of a bipedal rescue robot equipped with advanced modules. The main mission is to rescue and evacuate civilians from a city thrown into chaos by a Villain attack. The city is ravaged by destruction and various obstacles that require quick solutions, forcing players to choose the appropriate modules to clear paths, extinguish fires, or clear debris. All of this must be completed within a certain time limit, making every second precious.
